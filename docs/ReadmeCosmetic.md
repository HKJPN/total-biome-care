

## 📣 Cosmetic AI Analyzer

Simply take a photo of a cosmetic product’s full ingredient list and let Cosmetic AI Analyzer assess how the ingredients may affect the skin microbiome. General User Mode explains the function of each ingredient in clear language, while Healthcare Professional / Researcher Mode provides a more technical evaluation based on current scientific literature. Use it to gain a deeper understanding of the ingredients that come into contact with your skin.

---

## 📖 User Guide: Cosmetic AI Analyzer

Cosmetic AI Analyzer is a web-based service that uses AI to read an image of a cosmetic product’s full ingredient list and analyze the role of each ingredient and its potential effects on the resident skin microbiota.

### 📝 How to Use the App

1. **Review the Terms of Use**  
   Read the Terms of Use and select “I Agree.”

2. **Select your user type**  
   Choose either “Healthcare Professional / Researcher” or “Other / General User.”

3. **Upload an image**  
   Use the “Select / Take Photo” button to upload a photo of the cosmetic product’s **full ingredient list**, a formulation table, or similar information. The maximum file size is 5 MB.

4. **Start the analysis**  
   Select “Start Analysis” and wait for the results.


### 🔍 Analysis Modes

* **Other / General User Mode**  
  This mode quickly reads the ingredients and counts those that may affect the resident skin microbiota. It presents the functions and roles of all identified ingredients—such as moisturizers and preservatives—in a clear table.

* **Healthcare Professional / Researcher Mode**  
  This mode searches and cross-checks current English-language, peer-reviewed literature and provides a detailed discussion of potential effects on resident microorganisms such as *Staphylococcus epidermidis*. It also generates ingredient-level assessments using indicators such as 🔴, 🟠, and 🟡, together with supporting references and DOI links.

### ⚠️ Important Information

* This service is intended solely to provide general academic information. It does not provide medical diagnosis or medical advice.
* Consult a qualified physician or dermatologist if you have skin irritation, a skin disorder, or any other skin-related concern.
* Uploaded images are used only for analysis and are deleted after the analysis is completed.
* To protect the content, right-clicking, copying, and printing may be restricted on the results screen.
* To prevent excessive traffic, use is limited to 10 analyses per hour and 100 analyses per day.
