

## 📣 NutriLens AI (AI Meal & Nutrition Analyzer)

Simply take a photo of your meal and let NutriLens AI identify the food and analyze its nutritional balance, including calories, protein, fat, carbohydrates, dietary fiber, vitamins, and minerals. General Mode provides an easy-to-understand overview of the dish, its main ingredients, and practical nutrition tips, while Professional Mode generates detailed portion-weight estimates and a comprehensive nutrition report. Make your everyday meals easier—and more enjoyable—to understand at a glance.

## 📖 User Guide: NutriLens AI

NutriLens AI is a web-based service that uses AI to analyze photos of food, meals, and beverages and estimate their nutritional composition. It can analyze a wide range of meal images, including set meals, multiple dishes, meals arranged on a tray, boxed lunches, soups, rice, side dishes, desserts, and drinks.

1. **Select a language**  
   Choose either “日本語” or “English.” The default language may be selected automatically based on your browser settings.

2. **Select an analysis mode**  
   Choose either “General Mode (Portion Estimate & Overview)” or “Professional Mode (Detailed Analysis & Verification Record),” depending on your needs.

3. **Upload an image**  
   Select a photo containing food, a meal, or a beverage using the “Select a Meal Photo” button. PNG, JPEG, WebP, and HEIC files are supported. The recommended maximum file size is 5 MB.

4. **Start the analysis**  
   Select “Analyze Meal” and wait for the results. General Mode provides a concise overview, while Professional Mode performs a more detailed nutritional analysis.

### 🔍 Analysis Modes

* **General Mode (Portion Estimate & Overview)**  
  This mode estimates the name of the dish and identifies approximately three to five visible ingredients. It also provides practical nutrition tips and a rough estimate of dietary fiber content. It is ideal for quickly understanding the main nutritional characteristics of a meal.

* **Professional Mode (Detailed Analysis & Verification Record)**  
  This mode identifies the likely ingredients in the image and estimates the weight per serving, the expected margin of error, and the confidence level. It then calculates energy, protein, fat, carbohydrates, dietary fiber, fatty acids, vitamins, minerals, and other nutrients in greater detail. When objects such as spoons, chopsticks, rice bowls, or plate rims are visible, the app may use them as size references to produce more realistic weight estimates.

### 📊 Main Information Available in Professional Mode

* Basis of the estimate, including identified ingredients and estimated weights
* Average confidence level across all identified ingredients
* Energy, protein, fat, and carbohydrates
* Dietary fiber: total, soluble, and insoluble
* Percentage of energy from fat and protein
* Saturated fatty acids, n-3 fatty acids, n-6 fatty acids, and the n-3/n-6 ratio
* Vitamins A, B₁, B₂, C, and D, and folate
* Calcium, phosphorus, the calcium-to-phosphorus ratio, iron, magnesium, zinc, and potassium
* Assumptions used for cooking-related adjustments

### 💾 Saving Results

In Professional Mode, analysis results can be saved in Markdown format when needed. Enter the Markdown export password on the results screen and select “Save as MD.” The file name is generated automatically and includes the date and a sequential number.

### ⚠️ Important Information

* This service is intended to provide general information about meals and nutritional balance. It does not provide medical diagnoses, treatment, or individualized dietary counseling.
* Consult a physician or registered dietitian if you have diabetes, kidney disease, food allergies, dietary restrictions, are pregnant, or otherwise require careful dietary management.
* Weight and nutrient estimates based on images are subject to error. Depth, hidden ingredients, seasonings, oils, sauces, and cooking methods may not be determined accurately from a photograph alone.
* The app cannot analyze images that do not contain food, meals, or beverages. Take a clear photo in which the food is easy to see.
* Uploaded images are used only for analysis. Avoid including personal information or unnecessary background details in the image.
* In Professional Mode, right-clicking, copying, or printing from the results screen may be restricted to protect the content. Use the Markdown export function when you need to retain a record.
* To prevent excessive traffic, repeated use within a short period may be limited. If a usage-limit message appears, wait before trying again.
