

## 📣 Gut Microbiome AI Analyzer

Take a photo and let Gut Microbiome AI Analyzer assess how a food or ingredient may affect the gut microbiome. Using images of food packaging, ingredients, meals, supplements, or composition tables, the app evaluates potential gut-health effects based on peer-reviewed research. It provides both an easy-to-understand summary for general users and a detailed evidence report for healthcare professionals and researchers.

---

## 📖 User Guide: Gut Microbiome AI Analyzer

Gut Microbiome AI Analyzer is a web-based tool that uses AI to assess how foods, ingredients, supplements, and related products may affect the gut microbiota and the intestinal environment.

### 📝 How to Use the App

1. **Review the Terms of Use and select a mode**  
   Read the Terms of Use and select “I Agree.” Then choose either “Healthcare Professional / Researcher” or “General User,” depending on your intended use.

2. **Upload an image**  
   Use the “Select / Take Photo” button to upload an image of a **food ingredient list**, **food ingredient**, **prepared dish**, supplement, or a composition table from a research paper. The maximum file size is 5 MB. Quantitative assessment may be possible when the image includes ingredient amounts or weights; otherwise, the app may provide an approximate estimate based on the visible information.

3. **Start the analysis**  
   Select “Start Analysis” and wait for the results. The AI analyzes the ingredients and compares them with information from current research databases. Depending on the request, analysis may take approximately one to five minutes.


### 🔍 Analysis Modes

* 🟢 **General User Mode**
  * Summarizes the results in clear, accessible language with minimal technical terminology.
  * Uses intuitive indicators such as “Gut Health: ☆☆☆” and “Caution: ⚠” to show potential benefits or concerns and provides brief practical guidance.

* 📊 **Healthcare Professional / Researcher Mode**
  * Provides a detailed analysis of the evidence level and possible mechanisms of action for each ingredient, based primarily on English-language, peer-reviewed studies, with priority given to human research.
  * Generates a reference list containing verified publications with DOI links or URLs.

### ⚠️ Important Information

* This service searches for and presents academic information. It **does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or medical advice**.
* Consult a physician before using the information if you have an existing medical condition or any other health concern.
